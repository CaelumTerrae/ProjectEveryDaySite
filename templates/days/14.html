Intro

This is a project I have been working on for my Ancestry class at school! The project was to do a visualization of my family tree and write a small response.

Family Graph

As a computer scientist, when I hear the word "tree", I think of graphs. Trees are a particular type of graph, where any two vertices are connected by one path, and each node has two parents. In my family, where many individuals either do not know their parents because they were brought up as orphans, or where connections to family are separated by years and oceans, a better representation of the relationships in my family would be through a broader graph. I made a javascript visualization to show exactly the types of relationships.

Arrows point from a child to their mother, and dashed lines show that two individuals are married.
