Intro

I told you I was gonna do non-technical projects, and boy do I have projects. Today, I worked on a little bit of pixel art for the website, wrote out the outline for a pilot I am writing with Braedon Villano and made a humor piece about drinking pee. Let's get into it.

Favicons

Favicons are the little icons that show up next to your tab when you go on a website. They are not an essential part of a website by any means, but they are a nice little touch to add to polish one up. As one can imagine, they are really small (16 pixels by 16.) Emily (my girlfriend) is designing one for the site, but in the meantime, I thought I would design a couple as well. Here are the three that I came up with:

I'm pretty happy with the way these turned out. The first is supposed to be a succulent with a flower because why not, the second is a toolbox to represent the work I do on these projects everyday, and the last is the acronym of the website.

Pilot Writing

Braedon and I are super excited about this pilot and our work on it today. It's my first time writing a pilot, so I am conservative in my estimation about how good the first iteration will be, but even still, I can't wait to keep writing for it. The process of writing for this has definitely been very different from my previous writing experiences. I don't know if it's the co-writing aspect or the fact that I'm writing for a different medium than I usually do, but it is a lot of fun. Usually, I find the end-product of my writing to be the most enjoyable part, but am sour towards the process. This is different.

I won't spoil too much about the show itself, but it centers on the idea of fast food workers. That's all I'll say on that until we actually have the pilot done, which I'll probably post here if Braedon is cool with it. I'll end off this post with the comedy piece I wrote today.
